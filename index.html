About Us

Asaanti Films is a creative Oromo films agency based in Chicago, IL. We are composed of creative partners and experienced videographers.

Mission

What We Do.

We believe delivering high quality media products in Oromo is the only way we enrich our language today. In that effor we intend build a platform to connect our creative talents in our community to those goals. Film has become an amazing tool to evangelize ones culture and we intend to do the same for our people and our language.
